Welcome to LurkerFAQs!


LurkerFAQS is an archive of the GameFAQS message boards. Because there are thousands of boards on GameFAQS, only a selected few are archived.


Please visit here for a complete list of boards



You can search for a GameFAQs user and view their archived posts.




You are able to view archived topics even after they're purged on the original boards.
Let's say someone sends you a link to a topic:

http://www.gamefaqs.com/boards/213-nonstop-gaming-general/58411735

You go to the topic, but find out it has been purged. If the topic is on a board archived by LurkerFAQs, you can simply replace gamefaqs with lurkerfaqs in the URL to find an archived version of the topic.

http://www.lurkerfaqs.com/boards/213-nonstop-gaming-general/58411735
