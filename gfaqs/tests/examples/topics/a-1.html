SaintHero666 posted...

In Japan, do they sell manga in individual chapters or they only sell in volumes?

Chapters are released in Weekly shounen jump. Its a weekly (usually) magazine that is released on Mondays (we get our scans almost a week earlier) and contains all the Shounen Jump manag for the week.
After a series gets a bulk of chapters (usually 10, but it does vary sometimes) they release a volume of said series.
