Not as pretentious/frat-like as some other business majors but still walks around with an air of superiority because they feel like they've found the 'hidden gem' no one else has- a degree that mixes the relative simplicity of business majors with the job market of an Engineering degree. ONLY hangs out with other accounting majors.

Sports Marketing: A jock in HS who wasn't good enough to get on the college team but still smart/rich enough to go to college to learn, chooses Sports Marketing because he still has a dream of going pro even if it is a pro waterboy. Some will want to be a sports agent, next to zero will succeed. Joins a party frat. Goes to every sporting event, probably dating one of the uglier volleyball/tennis players. Does nothing but watch ESPN in his spare time. Wears A&F plus cargo shorts everywhere.

Communications: Female version of Sports Marketing. Doesn't know what the hell to do in college, so chooses Communications, because DURR COMMUNICATION IS VALUABLE FOR ANYTHING!! Physically, divides into two different types: The really quiet, average girl from HS who has only a few close friends or the loud, obnoxious, pretty whore who is in it for the party. Ends up working HR at some no name company.
